Tauranga

Tauranga CBD is the heart of Tauranga. It is filled to the brim with well known shops, amazing restaurants, innovative and fascinating attractions. Tauranga is a fast growing city ranked number one in urban growth in New Zealand and the CBD is the main heart of it all. Located on the other side of the Harbour Bridge away from the famous beaches of Mount Maunganui.

Attractions

In Tauranga there are many amazing things to visit from lavish restaurants overlooking The Wharf and peaceful harbour to inflatable water parks only available in the hot summer.
