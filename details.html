Details

This website was designed by Kaden Adlington for a school assessment, Its purpose it to provide tourists and visitors with information on places to go in the different areas of Tauranga City, it provides visitors with direct links to the main websites to and gives a few recommended suggestions for each area to encourage the visitors to look at them.

Colour Pallet

The colour pallet I used was a simple design that used a simple colours of greys and light grey and white and combined it with a pop of colour with the background, and a white box around the article to make it more readable.

Refinements

Throughout the entire production of the website I made a few minimal changes and 1 major change. A major change I made was deciding to shrink the size of my title on the article pages as shown here.

I felt that the main title on the article was to large and bulky and took up too much, so I decided to move it into the navigation bar so it didn't take up too much space and didn't stand out so much

Copyright

To make sure this Website is not violating copyright laws I have put effort into finding images that are creative commons or royalty free. Some images I have used state that they can be reused with credit

Testing

    Accessibility

    This website has a ALT text for every single image on the page so that it can be read by a screen reader it also uses a colour pallet that makes it readable to many and most colour-blind people.
